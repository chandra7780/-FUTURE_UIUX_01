🦷 Dental CRM Dashboard UI/UX Design

A modern and intuitive Dental CRM Dashboard designed to help dental clinics efficiently manage patients, appointments, leads, tasks, invoices, and reports—all from one place.

📌 Project Overview

This dashboard is designed with a clean and user-friendly interface that enables dental clinics to streamline their daily operations while providing an excellent user experience.

✨ Features
📊 Dashboard Overview
👥 Lead Management
🦷 Client Management
✅ Task Management
📅 Calendar Scheduling
📈 Reports & Analytics
💬 Messages
🧾 Invoice Management
👨‍💼 Team Management
⚙️ Settings
🔔 Notifications
📈 Revenue Tracking
📋 Lead Pipeline
📞 Follow-up Management
